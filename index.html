The Problem We Fix

Founders are running operations, sales, and delivery while marketing happens in fits and starts.

Misalignment between the offer, the audience, and the way leads convert creates friction that owner-time alone can't fix. Plans get made and stall. Campaigns start and don't finish. The team can't keep pace with the founder, and the founder can't slow down enough to teach. Foundations is the lightest version of the framework we use with $10M+ companies — built for the stage where the founder is still the system.

How We Work

Same framework as the parent CMO Systems brand. Smaller scope. We align three things — and the rest of the business follows.

People

Accountability, ownership, and clarity across the team. Who owns what stage of revenue.

Process

How revenue actually flows through your business. Where it leaks, where it stalls, where it compounds.

Performance

Demand quality, messaging, and conversion. The numbers that decide whether the system is working.

Who This Is For

Foundations is built for businesses between $500K and $10M in annual revenue. Two segments fit best:

  • Service businesses ($1M–$10M) where the founder is still selling, delivering, and managing marketing
  • Bootstrapped small businesses ($500K–$3M) ready to install marketing systems for the first time
  • Owners who feel the business has plateaued and the path forward isn't obvious
  • Teams with plans but no rhythm — campaigns that start and stall
  • Founders who need senior marketing leadership but can't justify a full-time hire
